Routiner

An execution operating system for daily routines that actively guides users through their day with sequential task flow, planning, and journaling.

Instead of being another passive todo list, Routiner acts like a guide that reduces decision fatigue by telling you what to focus on right now.

---

Vision

Most productivity apps store tasks.

Routiner drives execution.

Traditional flow:

Create task
↓
Forget task
↓
Feel guilty

Routiner flow:

Plan
↓
Execute
↓
Complete
↓
Reflect
↓
Improve

The goal is to make daily routines feel like progressing through levels rather than staring at an overwhelming task list.

---

Core Features

Sequential Task Flow

Complete one task and unlock the next step.

Example:

Wake up
↓
Typing Practice (Monkeytype opens)
↓
Deep Work Session
↓
Lunch
↓
Coding Session
↓
Evening Review
↓
Journal

---

Morning Planning

Quick daily planning:

- Main priority for the day
- Energy level
- Fill schedule gaps

---

Routine Templates

Create reusable routines:

- Morning routine
- Work routine
- Evening routine
- Study routine

---

End of Day Reflection

Simple journaling system:

- What went well?
- What went wrong?
- Tomorrow's focus

---

URL Launch Support

Tasks can optionally launch URLs automatically.

Examples:

- Monkeytype
- YouTube playlists
- Notes
- Documentation
- Learning resources
